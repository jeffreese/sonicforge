# One-Shot Sample Attribution

All samples in this directory are sourced from [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org)
under the [Creative Commons 0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)
public domain dedication. CC0 does not technically require attribution, but
crediting the original authors is good practice — and this file documents
exactly what's committed to the repo and where it came from.

If you're a sample author listed below and would like your attribution
adjusted, please open an issue on the SonicForge repository.

## Processing notes

- Files in `fx/` were transcoded from their original WAV/AIFF/MP3 formats to
  Opus (in an OGG container) at ~128 kbps to shrink the committed footprint
  ~10× without audible loss. The originals can be re-downloaded from the
  Freesound links below if raw assets are needed.
- Two snares (`cvltiv8r__brass-ludwig-snare.ogg` and `cryanrautha__ganon-snare.ogg`)
  are cropped to a single transient from the original multi-hit files and
  transcoded to Opus, also to save space.
- All other samples (`kicks/`, `hats/`, `claps/`, and three small `snares/`)
  are committed as-is in their original format since they were already small
  enough.
